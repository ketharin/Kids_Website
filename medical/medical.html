Healthy habits

We all have habits but are they healthy habits? With child obesity on the rise, it is critical that we are teaching our little ones healthy habits from a young age. Covid-19 Awareness

You may have noticed lots of adults talking about a 'coronavirus'.There is a new kind of this virus that has spread around the world. Mostly it makes people cough and feel like they have a bad cold; sometimes it can make people feel tired. Some people who have other health problems may become more sick from it and may need to be looked after in hospital.
